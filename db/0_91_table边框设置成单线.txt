<html><head></head><body><p>有两种方法。</p><ul><li>第一种方法：就是利用table标签<code>cellspacing=0</code>属性来实现。</li></ul><p><code>cellspacing</code>是内边框和外边框的距离，这种方法实现的<strong>看起来</strong>是单实线，其实是<strong>内边框线和外边框线组合成的实线</strong>。</p><p>例：</p><figure class="highlight html"><table><tbody><tr><td class="gutter"><pre><span class="line">1</span><br/><span class="line">2</span><br/><span class="line">3</span><br/></pre></td><td class="code"><pre><span class="line"><span class="tag">&lt;<span class="name">table</span> <span class="attr">cellspacing</span>=<span class="string">&#34;0&#34;</span> <span class="attr">border</span>=<span class="string">&#34;1px&#34;</span>&gt;</span></span><br/><span class="line">    ...</span><br/><span class="line"><span class="tag">&lt;/<span class="name">table</span>&gt;</span></span><br/></pre></td></tr></tbody></table></figure><ul><li>第二种方法是利用css的表格<code>border-collapse</code>属性来实现。</li></ul><figure class="highlight css"><table><tbody><tr><td class="gutter"><pre><span class="line">1</span><br/><span class="line">2</span><br/><span class="line">3</span><br/></pre></td><td class="code"><pre><span class="line"><span class="selector-tag">table</span>{</span><br/><span class="line">    <span class="attribute">border-collapse</span>: collapse;</span><br/><span class="line">}</span><br/></pre></td></tr></tbody></table></figure><blockquote><p>我更喜欢第二种方法。第一种方法适用于table较少，不考虑可维护的情况。否则，它会让HTML源文件变得混乱。</p></blockquote></body></html>
